WHO AM I

ABOUT ME

Video games are a part of my life for as long as I can remember. I started with the PlayStation 1 and from that moment I knew that world would captivate me forever. There's a Spyro game, "The Legend of Spyro: The Dragon's Force", that holds a special place in my heart. Today, my favorite game is Red Dead Redemption 2: an open world that, for me, is a masterpiece in every sense.

That passion is what led me to want to be on the other side, not just playing, but building. What motivates me most about development is the entire process—seeing how an idea takes shape, how each piece fits together, and reaching that moment when the project is finished, works, and looks exactly as you imagined. That final result is what drives me.

I like all genres, but open worlds are the ones I'm most passionate about, the freedom, the scale, the feeling that there's something new around every corner.
